About Us

A community of curious minds

The Dr. G.W. Williams Science & Discovery Club brings together students who love exploring the world through experiment and inquiry. Whether you're building circuits, debating climate data, or prepping for a national olympiad — there's a place here for you.

What We Do

Hands-on Experiments

Forget the textbook — roll up your sleeves. Weekly lab sessions let members design and run real experiments, from electrochemistry to optics.

Every Wednesday

STEM Competitions

Compete in Science Olympiad, regional fairs, and robotics challenges. Past teams have placed at the provincial level — your turn to shine.

Regional & National

Peer Mentorship

Senior members mentor younger students in science and math. Log volunteer hours, build leadership skills, and give back to the school community.

Volunteer Hours

  Field trips, guest lecturers & special workshops throughout the year.

Why Join?

Build Real Skills

Gain hands-on experience in critical thinking, lab technique, leadership, and teamwork — skills universities actually want to see.

Boost Your Application

STEM competition experience and club executive roles stand out on university and scholarship applications. We have alumni at top programs.

Find Your People

Connect with fellow science-passionate students in a welcoming, collaborative environment. Many of our best friendships started here.

Win & Achieve

Represent your school. Our members regularly place in regional and national STEM competitions with real prizes and recognition.
